# AutoStream-Marketing | Design Standards (v8.1-Refined)

## Visual Identity
- **Pallets**: Deep voids (#000000) with Electric Blue (#0066FF) and Vibrant Purple (#CC00FF) gradients.
- **Glassmorphism**: 
  - Backdrop Blur: `20px`.
  - Border: `1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`.
  - Opacity: `0.7`.
- **Typography**: Inter (Body), Outfit (Headings).

## UI Elements
- **3D Glass Medallions**: Icons with depth and inner glows.
- **Animations**: Soft eases, 300ms duration standard.
- **Tone**: Professional, High-End, Autonomous.

## Copywriting
- Always use Australian English:
  - `visualise` instead of `visualize`.
  - `optimise` instead of `optimize`.
  - `colour` instead of `color`.
  - `customise` instead of `customize`.
